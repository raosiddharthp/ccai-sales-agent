Sales Orchestration: Conversational Traces
SURFACING AGENTIC REASONING, RULE ENFORCEMENT & DYNAMIC CPQ LOGIC

These traces demonstrate the Omni-CCAI Reasoning Engine. Unlike basic chatbots, this agent maintains a complex session state, evaluating every user utterance against the MedTech Rules Engine. Each trace highlights the intersection of natural language, clinical validation, and proactive revenue generation.

Scenario 1: High-Resolution MRI (Rule-Gated Upsell)

User: "I need to configure a 3T MRI system for our research wing."

[Orchestrator]: Intent: 'product_config' | Entity: 'modality:MRI', 'strength:3T'
[Logic_Engine]: Checking Dependency: 3T MRI requires 'Premium Research' Tier.
[Upsell_Trigger]: AOV optimization: Facility with 3T frequently requires 'AI Diagnostics Suite'.

Agent: "Excellent choice. A 3T system provides the resolution needed for high-end research. To enable the 3T magnet, I've upgraded your configuration to the Premium Research Tier (+$600k). Also, 82% of research sites with 3T systems include our AI Diagnostics Suite to automate tumor detection. Shall I add that to your quote?"

User: "Yes, add the AI suite."

[Logic_Engine]: Appending 'AI_Diagnostics_Suite' (+$250,000).
[Safety_Check]: MRI modality detected. Automatically injecting 'Quench_Pipe' (+$80,000) for compliance.

Agent: "Added. I've also included the mandatory Quench Pipe installation (+$80k) required for MRI safety compliance. Your current capital total is $2,730,000. Would you like a formal quote summary?"

Scenario 2: CT Scanner (Conflict Resolution)

User: "We want the 256-slice CT scanner, but keep the Standard performance tier to save budget."

[Conflict_Detected]: 256-slice requires 'Premium Research' Tier. User requested 'Standard'.
[Resolution_Strategy]: Inform user of constraint + offer nearest valid alternative (128-slice / Advanced Tier).

Agent: "I understand the budget focus. However, the 256-slice scanner requires the technical infrastructure of the Premium Research tier. If we stay on a tighter budget, I recommend the 128-slice configuration with the Advanced Tier. This still offers high-throughput for ER use but reduces the capital adder by 50%. How does that sound?"

Strategic Alignment

By surfacing these reasoning steps, Omni-CCAI ensures SOX-compliant traceability for every quote generated. This prevents "Black Box" errors in regulated medical sales and provides the CFO's office with an immutable audit trail of why specific add-ons were included.
